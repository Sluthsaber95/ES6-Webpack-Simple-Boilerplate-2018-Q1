Using webpack-dev-middleware

webpack-dev-middleware is a wrapper that will emit files
processed by webpack to a server.
This is used in webpack-dev-server internally, however
it's available as a separate package to allow more custom
setups if desired.
We'll take a look at an example that combines
webpack-dev-middleware with an express server.
